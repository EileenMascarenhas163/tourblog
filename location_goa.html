Recommended destinations for you

Calangute Beach

Calangute is a vibrant beach resort in North Goa, where you can enjoy the sun, sand, and sea. The beach is one of the longest and busiest in Goa, offering a variety of water sports, beach shacks, and nightlife options. You can also explore the colourful streets of Calangute, where you can find local handicrafts, souvenirs, and cuisine. Calangute is also a cultural hub, with a rich history and heritage. You can visit the St. Alex Church, the Kerkar Art Complex, and the Museum of Goa, or listen to the famous Konkani song 'Calangute' by Lorna Cordeiro. Calangute is a place where you can experience the best of Goa's charm and energy.

Panji

Panaji is the capital of the Indian state of Goa and the headquarters of North Goa district. Previously, it was the territorial capital of the former Portuguese India. It lies on the banks of the Mandovi river estuary in the Tiswadi sub-district. With a population of 114,759 in the metropolitan area, Panaji is Goa's largest urban agglomeration, ahead of Margao and Mormugao.

Silvassa

Silvassa is a city that offers a blend of nature, culture, and adventure for travelers who want to explore a lesser-known destination in India. Silvassa is the capital of the union territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, which was once a Portuguese colony. You can witness the traces of the colonial past in the architecture, cuisine, and festivals of the region. You can also enjoy the scenic beauty of the Western Ghats, the wildlife sanctuaries, and the water sports at the Daman Ganga river. Silvassa is a place where you can relax, rejuvenate, and rediscover yourself.

Varca

If you are looking for a serene and scenic beach getaway in Goa, Varca Beach might be the perfect choice for you. This pristine beach, located in the south of Goa, offers a tranquil and relaxing atmosphere, away from the crowds and noise of the more popular beaches. You can enjoy the soft white sand, the swaying palm trees, and the clear blue waters of the Arabian Sea, while soaking up the sun or indulging in some water sports. Varca Beach also has a charming cultural side, with the white-washed Our Lady of Gloria church, a historic landmark that reflects the Portuguese influence in Goa. Varca Beach is a destination that will make you fall in love with Goa's natural and cultural beauty.

Arambol

Arambol is a traditional fisherman village, located approximately a 90 minutes drive from Dabolim Airport within the Pernem administrative region of North Goa, India. The beach attracts many international tourists, mainly during the winter season between November and March.

Candolim

Candolim is a beach lover's paradise in North Goa, where you can enjoy the sun, sand, and sea. Candolim Beach is one of the longest and cleanest beaches in Goa, and it offers a variety of water sports such as jet skiing, parasailing, banana boat rides, and more. You can also relax at the beach shacks, indulge in some local cuisine, or shop for souvenirs at the nearby markets. Candolim is also a historical town, as it was the first village to embrace Christianity in India. You can visit the Aguada Fort, the Candolim Church, and the Sinquerim Lighthouse to learn more about the colonial past of Goa. Candolim is a perfect destination for a fun-filled and memorable vacation in Goa.
